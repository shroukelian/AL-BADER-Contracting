The Story of MKT and Our Commitment to Quality

Founded in 2019, we drive excellence and trust in specialized building protection.

Who We Are

MKT was founded in 2019 by Mr. Mohammed Thuwaini. We uphold the values upon which we were established, maintaining a commitment to high-quality performance and outstanding customer service.

We have witnessed significant growth in the building protection sector across Saudi Arabia, successfully establishing MKT as a prominent industrial contracting firm in Riyadh. Our acquired experience allows us to provide a superior level of service and craftsmanship.
