Our Specialized Engineering & Protection Services

We deliver comprehensive solutions to ensure the durability and longevity of your structures.

1. Steel Sandblasting (Surface Preparation)

We provide high-quality sandblasting to remove rust, impurities, and old paint, preparing surfaces perfectly for durable protective coatings.

  • Prepares steel structures, bridges, and tanks.
  • Ensures a clean surface ready for final treatment.

2. Protective Coatings for Steel

We excel in applying industrial coating systems that protect steel structures from corrosion. We use high-resistance paints for long-term durability.

  • Applied with modern spraying techniques for even distribution.
  • Strong protection that lasts for many years.

3. Industrial Epoxy Flooring & Concrete Repair

We provide all types of chemical and abrasion-resistant epoxy flooring systems, including concrete crack repair and resurfacing for enhanced durability.

  • Smooth, easy-to-clean surfaces for industrial use.
  • Utilizing certified materials for added surface strength.

4. Tank Lining and Sealing (Steel & Concrete)

Integrated services for lining and insulating industrial, water, and oil tanks using corrosion and leak-resistant epoxy and polymer coatings.

  • Protects tanks from rust and corrosion for years.
  • Solutions customized to the stored fluid type.

5. Fire Stopping (Compartmentation)

We provide certified fire stopping systems to prevent the spread of flames and smoke between walls and ceilings, executed strictly according to Saudi Civil Defense requirements.

  • Ensures building compliance and safety standards.
  • Aims for safe evacuation during emergencies.

6. Road Marking and Ground Lines

Execution of all road and parking lot marking using high-quality, reflective, and weather-resistant paints for long-term visibility.

  • Execution adheres to Saudi traffic standards and precision.
  • Goal: Safe and organized road environments.

7. Waterproofing & Thermal Insulation

Effective solutions to protect buildings from water leakage and humidity, along with thermal insulation systems that reduce energy consumption and improve indoor comfort.

  • Reduces energy consumption and improves comfort.
  • Offers long-term warranties on insulation quality.

8. Fire Retardant Intumescent Coatings

Integrated solutions for certified fire-resistant paints. Our specialized coatings react to heat to form an insulating layer, protecting the metal structure for extended periods.

  • Provides fire resistance for several hours.
  • Guarantees full compliance with Saudi Civil Defense requirements.
