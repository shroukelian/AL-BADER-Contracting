Our Specialized Services in Coatings and Protection.

We offer integrated solutions with global standards that guarantee quality, precision, and completion in every project.

1. Epoxy Floors & Concrete Repair

We provide all types of chemical and abrasion-resistant epoxy floors, and offer repair solutions for cracks and erosion in concrete.

  • Smooth, easy-to-clean surfaces with a modern and elegant appearance.
  • Use of certified materials that provide additional strength and durability to surfaces.

2. Protective Coatings for Steel

We excel in applying coating systems that protect steel structures from corrosion. We use industrial paints highly resistant to moisture and chemicals.

  • Use the latest spraying techniques to ensure homogeneous distribution.
  • The final result: strong protection that lasts for many years.

3. Steel Sandblasting (Abrasive Blasting)

We provide sandblasting services with the highest quality standards to remove rust and impurities. We prepare surfaces perfectly to ensure durable adhesion of protective coatings.

  • Preparation of steel structures, bridges, and tanks.
  • We ensure a clean surface fully ready for any final coating.

4. Tank Lining and Insulation (Steel and Concrete)

We offer integrated services for insulating and lining industrial and water tanks. We use epoxy materials and polymeric coatings resistant to corrosion and leakage.

  • Protection of tanks from rust and corrosion for many years.
  • Customized solutions based on the type of liquid stored and temperature.

5. Fire Protection (Insulation & Containment)

We offer specialized coating solutions to protect steel structures from high temperatures, using internationally certified insulating materials that prevent the loss of structural integrity when exposed to fire.

  • The coating is applied in measured thicknesses to ensure effective thermal insulation and a long resistance period, granting structures the highest levels of safety.
  • We use precise application techniques and a specialized team to ensure reliable performance and high sustainability in various industrial and commercial projects.

6. Road and Ground Markings

We execute all road and parking lot marking works using high-quality reflective and weather-resistant paints.

  • We ensure precise measurements and adherence to Saudi traffic standards during execution.
  • Our goal is a safe and organized road environment.

7. Water and Thermal Insulation

We provide effective solutions to protect buildings from water leakage and moisture, and thermal insulation systems that reduce energy consumption. We use high-quality polymeric and bituminous materials.

  • Reduced energy consumption and maintenance of internal comfort.
  • We offer long-term guarantees on the quality of insulation.

8. Fire Protection Paints

We offer integrated solutions for certified fire-resistant paints, where our specialized coatings react to heat to form an insulating layer that protects the steel structure.

  • Paints provide a fire resistance time of up to many hours.
  • We ensure full compliance with Civil Defense requirements.
